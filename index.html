About Us

Doc-Tour is an organization that specializes in providing curative services in all medical specialties. It also provides high-quality health care in an atmosphere full of safety, care and complete confidentiality which correspond to international standards to provide the highest level of services to patients worldwide.

V

Our Vision

At Doc-Tour we strive to be the pioneering leaders in providing integrated health care services in the Middle East by commitment to the best international quality and standards, providing all necessary medical, surgical and therapeutic services, not only for expatriates from the countries of the Middle East, but also worldwide. In addition to improving the levels of health services to be the first and primary choice for our clients, and to maintain our customers' trust, which we consider to be the most important element of our success. We believe that giving the desired care and importance to recreational tourism along with treatment programs is what makes Egypt a primary medical-tour destination for all different groups and ages, and at all times of the year.

G

Our Goal

we aim to raise the efficiency and level of different treatment services inside Egypt, which includes conducting the necessary surgical operations and treatment of various diseases besides entertaining recreational programs. This service offers the privilege of saving your time, effort, money and eventually coming up with an economic equation that makes medical services accessible to the largest number of patients worldwide and also to the patients from the Middle East. We also aim to emphasize to the largest possible number of individuals the strong role that Egypt offers in the various fields of medicine, and revitalize the concept of treatment and recreation, and highlight its value and asset, in addition to directing the expert specialists and physicians that are experienced.
